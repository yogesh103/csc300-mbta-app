About us

This is a webapp built using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.js) that provides information and services for the Massachusetts Bay Transportation Authority (MBTA).

Our mission today is to make it easy for commuters and visitors to navigate the MBTA system by providing up-to-date information on schedules, delays, and other important news.

Want to buy ticket for subway? Click "book tickets" on top left corner.
